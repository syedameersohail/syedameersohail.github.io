Key Projects

Short Term Risk Assessment for Proactive Maintainence

Data Scientist @ Affinity Water, Hatfield

Currently, I am working on developing system for forecasting pipe failures in water networks across the north parts of London and areas in the south of England. This project focuses on saving water by identifying and preventing potential pipe bursts, which can lead to significant water loss, pollute near by water streams. Burst pipes can also negatively impact people’s daily lives by flooding roads, causing damage to infrastructure, and creating hazardous conditions. I am happy that I am working on project that would make a meaningful impact on communities, protect the environment and optimise company assets through proactive risk assessment and maintenance strategies using AI and Machine learning.
